Happy Independence Day!

14th August is a day of freedom and pride for Pakistanis all around the world. Let's celebrate this day with patriotism and unity.

Pakistan

Pakistan is an independent country that came into being on 14th August 1947. It is officially known as the Islamic Republic of Pakistan and its capital city is Islamabad. The country is blessed with beautiful mountains, rivers, and rich natural resources. Urdu is the national language, and Islam is the religion of the majority. Pakistanis take pride in their culture, traditions, and their hard-earned independence.
